DCS Line Representation of Blockchain Platforms

Choosing the right blockchain platform for your project can be a daunting task. This representation simplifies the complexity by intuitively displaying characteristics of different blockchains.

To increase the scale of the platform, you need to make trade-offs and adjust the degree of decentralization and consistency. Play around with the diagram by dragging the control points towards the origin to find a suitable blockchain for your project.

For example, at default we have PoW based Bitcoin/Ethereum with full decentralization and max consistency, but with low scalability. Try to reduce both decentralization and consistency by a notch. Now we have NEO with higher scalability.

  • High Scale: high throughput, high capacity, low latency
  • High Consistency: all nodes see the same data at the same time
  • Full Decentralization: anyone can join in as a validator
